# Bike Share Deep-Dive Analysis

Detailed analysis of Bike Share Toronto usage specifically within the Beach BIA and surrounding waterfront.

## Themes
- **Dwell Time (`Beaches_Bikes_Dwell.qmd`)**: Analysis of the popular times of arrivals and departures from The Beaches on bikes, indicating visitor stay duration.
- **Clustering (`Beaches_Bikes_Clusters.qmd`)**: K-means & spatial clustering of bike trips to determmine usage profiles.
- **Heatmaps (`Beaches_Bikes_Heatmap.qmd`)**: Visualizations of trip density and popular bike stations.
