From this output, it appears that cluster 1 mainly comprises of longer trips all throughout the day, with the average start time at about 3:30pm. We also have a very large concentration of casual to annual members, indicating that this cluster is likely a good representation of leisure trips. This is a strong assumption as we are likely missing other leisure trips that are being captured by the other clusters, however, it is a sizeable sample to proceed with.
